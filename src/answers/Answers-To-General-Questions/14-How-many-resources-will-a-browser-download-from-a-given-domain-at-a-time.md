# How many resources will a browser download from a given domain at a time ?

| Browser | Concurrent Requests |
| --- | --- |
| IE7 | 2 |
| IE8 | 6 |
| IE10 | 8 |
| Safari5 | 6 |
| Chrome | 6 |
| Firefox | 8 |

## What are the exceptions ?
Above works only works for HTTP 1.1

SPDY, HTTP/2 may vary
